Steps to Deploy Apiman to Fabric8 on Vagrant

22 Sep 2015

  1. Obtain the Fabric8 installer

git clone git@github.com:fabric8io/fabric8-installer.git

  1. Obtain and install the OpenShift client tools, version 1.0.6 or newer, from https://github.com/openshift/origin/releases. Pick the archive with platform architecture right for your machine and simply extract and copy them to a location so that they are on your PATH. .
